Hi! Welcome to PenMouse S – control your phone using the S Pen.

First and foremost: PenMouse S is an experimental app and uses the S Pen Remote SDK, which is no longer supported by Samsung. This means there may be bugs, connection drops, or the app might not work at all with your device. I apologize for any issues, but unfortunately, some of them are beyond my ability to fix. If the app doesn’t work on your phone now, it most likely never will.

Please carefully follow all the steps required to prepare your phone for using Air Mouse, otherwise the app may not function properly. Also, make sure your device supports S Pen Air Actions.

Compatible devices:

  • Galaxy Note10
  • S20 Ultra - S24 Ultra
  • Galaxy Tabs with S Pen remote support
Please Note: The S25 Ultra no longer supports Bluetooth in the S Pen.

Before enabling Air Mouse for the first time, you’ll be asked for:

  • Notification permission – The app sends silent status updates, like whether the cursor is active, hidden, or asleep. You can change this later in the settings.
  • Accessibility service access – This is required to display the cursor icon over all apps and system UI, and to simulate finger taps on the screen.
    PenMouse S DOES NOT read or share any of your screen content.

Quick usage guide:

After completing all the steps, removing the S Pen from your device, waiting for it to connect successfully to your phone, and starting the Air Mouse, a cursor will appear on the screen. Moving the stylus will move the cursor, and pressing the S Pen button will simulate a finger tap on the screen.

Due to system limitations, PenMouse S does not support hover effects or right-click actions. You can only simulate taps and gestures.

If you put the S Pen down and the cursor stops moving, it will hide after 10 seconds (this time can be changed in the settings). To make the cursor reappear, simply move the S Pen.

However, if the cursor remains hidden, after one minute the S Pen’s gyroscope will turn off to save battery. The cursor will no longer respond to movement. To reactivate the gyroscope, press the S Pen button – this behavior can also be changed in the settings.

If you've enabled notifications, the app will show a status notification with the current state of the S Pen (active, hidden, sleeping). From the notification, you can open the app or stop the Air Mouse.

Don’t forget to check the settings and customize the app to your preferences.
